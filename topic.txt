**Problem Statement:**
Exploring the use of spot VMs in dev clusters to reduce costs.

**Work Involved:**
- Set up and configure spot VMs in dev clusters.
- Monitor performance and cost benefits.
- Document findings and resolve issues.

**Test Criteria:**
- Successful deployment without disruptions.
- Performance equal to or better than regular VMs.
- Cost savings observed.
- Stability maintained.

**Dependencies:**
- Coordination with dev and ops teams.
