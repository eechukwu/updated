**Description**
Setup policy controller for the GKE Fleet Clusters to enforce security and compliance policies.


**Problem Statement**
Ensure security and compliance policies are enforced across all GKE Fleet Clusters consistently.


**Work Involved**
- Review current security and compliance setup.
- Implement policy controller.
- Test policy enforcement.
- Document the process.


**Test Criteria**
- Validate proper policy enforcement across clusters.
- Ensure accurate and timely propagation of policy changes.
- Monitor and resolve any enforcement issues.


**Dependencies**
- Access to all GKE Fleet Clusters.

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Policy controller set up and validated.
- Process documented.
